About Mr.Clean

Our Mission

Our focus is keeping our customers
happy by providing excellent service with
an emphasis on healthy product.

Core Goals

-Remove harmful chemicals from carpets
-Improve indoor health through our products and services
-Improve indoor air quality
-Educate our clients on how to best care for their flooring
-Create longterm customers

Origin

Mr.Clean was founded in 1971 in Sandpoint, Idaho.
In 2017 Mr.Clean was relocated to
Boise, Idaho with intentions of expansion.
